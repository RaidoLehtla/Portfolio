About Me

Hello! I'm Raido, originally from Pärnu, now a resident of Tallinn for the past 8 years. My journey has been a diverse one—from restoring wooden doors and windows to dedicating the last 5 years to the art of locksmithing. Around 1.5 years ago, I stumbled upon a new passion that ignited my curiosity—the world of coding. Exploring this dynamic realm, I've found joy in crafting digital solutions and solving problems with lines of code. Now, armed with a growing skill set and a fervent enthusiasm for development, I'm eagerly seeking an entry-level opportunity to apply my coding expertise. I'm ready to contribute, learn, and grow in a professional environment, bringing dedication and a fresh perspective to the table.
